What types of apparel do you manufacture?

We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of apparel including but not limited to t-shirts, hoodies, sweatshirts, polo shirts, jackets, and custom-designed clothing for various purposes like corporate events, promotional merchandise, sports teams, etc.

Can I request designs or branding on apparel?

Yes, we offer custom design services! Our team can work with your specific requirements to create personalized designs or incorporate your branding/logo onto the apparel.

What is the minimum order quantity for custom apparel?

Clothing, we specialize in crafting high-quality garments with a minimum order quantity of 50 pieces. This allows our clients the flexibility to meet their unique needs while benefiting from our efficient production process. Whether it's custom designs or bulk orders for various purposes, we are dedicated to delivering stylish and durable products, fostering strong partnerships with our clients.

How ling does it take to manufacture custom apparel?

Production time depends on the complexity of the order and the quantity requested. Typically, it takes 3 weeks from the approval of the final design and order confirmation.

Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

No, we don't offer custom samples because our bulk process includes a pre-production sample which is a mandatory step.
